Love's Dark Abyss - The Romeo and Juliet of the Caribbean

(Based on the true story of Lover's Leap)

Mizzy and Tunkey, enslaved lovers on neighbouring plantations, were torn apart by Richard Chardley, Mizzy's “owner„. Chardley was jealous of their love and planned to have Tunkey sold to another plantation far away from his lover. The couple heard of his plot and decided to run away together. They were then chased by Chardley's men when they came upon a cliff. There was no escape. They had no choice but literally take the leap of faith. Legends say their story continues in the skies.
